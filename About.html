The Adiyogi Shiva Statue in Coimbatore, India is a magnificent 112-foot-tall statue of the Hindu God Shiva. It was built in 2017 to honor the Hindu deity and is the world’s largest bust sculpture. Located in the Isha Yoga Center, the statue is a popular tourist attraction and a sacred pilgrimage site for Hindus. Here are 10 things to do when visiting the Adiyogi Shiva Statue in Coimbatore, India. 1. Take a Tour of the Statue: The Adiyogi Shiva Statue is a breathtaking sight to behold. Visitors can take a guided tour of the statue and learn about its history and symbolism. The tour also includes a visit to the meditation hall and a chance to meditate in the presence of the statue. 2. Visit the Isha Yoga Center: The Isha Yoga Center is a spiritual retreat located near the Adiyogi Shiva Statue. Visitors can take part in various activities such as yoga classes, meditation sessions, and spiritual lectures. The center also has a range of shops and restaurants. 3. Attend a Festival or Event: The Adiyogi Shiva Statue is the side of many festivals and events throughout the year. Visitors can attend the Mahashivratri Festival, which is celebrated in honor of Lord Shiva, or the International Yoga Festival, which is held annually. 4. Attend an Arti Ceremony: Every evening, an arti ceremony is held at the Adiyogi Shiva Statue. During the ceremony, devotees offer flowers and prayers to the deity. Visitors can join in the ceremony and experience the spiritual atmosphere. 5. See the Light and Sound Show: Every evening, the Adiyogi Shiva Statue is illuminated with a spectacular light and sound show. The show is a visual and auditory feast, featuring music, lasers, and projections of images onto the statue. 6. Take a Hike: The Isha Yoga Center is surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills. Visitors can take a hike in the area and experience the natural beauty of the landscape. 7. Visit the Adiyogi Shiva Cave: The Adiyogi Shiva Cave is located near the Adiyogi Shiva Statue. The cave is said to be the place where Lord Shiva meditated and attained enlightenment. Visitors can explore the cave and experience its spiritual atmosphere. 8. Visit the Adiyogi Shiva Temple: The Adiyogi Shiva Temple is located near the Isha Yoga Center. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is a popular pilgrimage site for Hindus. Visitors can explore the temple and see the beautiful carvings and sculptures. 9. Take a Boat Ride: Visitors can take a boat ride on the lake near the Isha Yoga Center. The ride provides a peaceful and calming experience, as visitors enjoy the beauty of the lake and the surrounding landscape. 10. Shop at the Local Markets: The Isha Yoga Center is located near a number of local markets. Visitors can take a stroll through the markets and buy souvenirs, handmade crafts, and local produce. Visiting the Adiyogi Shiva Statue in Coimbatore, India is an unforgettable experience. Whether you’re looking for a spiritual journey or a fun-filled day trip, there’s something for everyone at this iconic site.
